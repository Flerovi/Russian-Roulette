Welcome to GoreBox!

Dive into the adrenaline-fueled world of GoreBox, an action-packed sandbox game that harmoniously fuses relentless action with unlimited creativity. Here, you're equipped with an extensive array of weapons and explosives, as well as a special piece of equipment that stands out from the rest: the Reality Crusher.

Reality Crusher: The Power in Your Hands

The Reality Crusher is no ordinary tool. It gives you the power to spawn, modify, and annihilate any spawnable entity in the game. With this device, you are not just a bystander in the world of GoreBox - you are the master of your chaotic journey.

Interact and Adapt with Dynamic Settings

As a player, you share the same damage system as the dynamic, physics-based ragdolls in the game. These unique interactions turn every encounter into a thrilling experience as you balance between sowing chaos and self-preservation.

Express Yourself

Express your personality in GoreBox with customizable skins and spawnable accessories like practical armor, hats, and masks. Why stop at yourself? Even the goredolls can mirror your style!

Role-play, Chat, and Trade

While GoreBox lacks formal role-playing (RP) features, it paves the way for RP through its chat system and trading functionalities. Communicate with other players using whisper and emote commands, and trade using the in-game currency. Remember: death leads to losing a fraction of your money, raising the stakes of every encounter.

Cross-platform Play

GoreBox shines with its cross-platform compatibility. Fly helicopters, orchestrate epic NPC battles, or explore the world with friends - the fun is always at your fingertips, regardless of the device you're using.
